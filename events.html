Dramatics

An annual event where each house performs an original skit on a given topic. Every member of each house is anxious to win and it is a time when the house truly comes together.

School Play

The School Play is another acting event, but it is not a inter-house competition The school play is a much grander event and is usually based on other famous movies, plays or stories.

Sports Day

Sports Day is the day where the students of our school show off their atletic prowess. The houses also show their co-ordination and team work in marching and tug-of-war.

Encore

It is an event that takes place in December and is like a music fest, where students of the Senior School perform.
